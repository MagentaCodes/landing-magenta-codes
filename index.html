About Us

Magenta Codes is a community-based in Sweden, created by expat women that are entering or already are in the field of technology. As immigrant women, we were facing different barriers to access information, education, and job opportunities.

We got together to exchange ideas and witness the challenges that all immigrant women or non-binary immigrants faced in a new country where they did not speak the language and there few opportunities to be hired as technology beginners. Our goals are inclusion, support, development, and gender equality in the current tech revolution and economical climate. This is in alignment with the UN sustainable development goals (Goal 5), related to gender equality and we are also under the wings of Women Tech Makers.

Our goals

Become part of a community of 335 members passionate about creating opportunities to make a more diverse and inclusive technology industry.

Help empowers women in their tech careers by providing access to curated resources and events, as well as information and tools.

Create events and programs to share knowledge strategically with women at all professional levels through the collaboration of tech experts.

The benefits are as limitless as the opportunities. You will strengthen yourself mentally and physically, meet new friends, and make connections that share common interests, values, and perspectives. You could also find new ways to relieve stress and find your passion.

Healthy!

If you are considering a new career or advancing in your own path, volunteering can help you gain experience in your area of interest and expand your network. Even if you are not planning to change careers, volunteering gives you the opportunity to practice important skills and increase your knowledge.

Advance Your Career!

It is very rewarding to help others and feel that your actions are making a difference for the better in the world. Happiness is just one of the many benefits of volunteering and a fun and easy way to explore your interests and passions.
